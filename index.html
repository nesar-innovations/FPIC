The BEC Medium

By cooling induction elements to a Bose-Einstein Condensate state, the FPIC eliminates atomic resistance. Current flows as a superfluid, ensuring 100% thermal efficiency.

Meissner Suspension

The internal rotor utilizes Type-II Superconductors to achieve quantum levitation. Operating in a total vacuum, the system removes all mechanical friction and air drag.

Lorentz Induction

The chamber syncs with the Planetary Magnetosphere. As the Earth rotates, the relative motion induces a constant DC voltage without the need for external fuel sources.
